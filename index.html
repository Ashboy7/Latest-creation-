1. ChatGPT Search by OpenAI

Combining conversational AI with live internet results, ChatGPT Search lets users chat, ask follow-ups, and get dynamic responses, including articles, charts, and summaries. Ideal for research, coding, and writing help.

2. Google Gemini

Google's flagship AI integrates deeply with search. Gemini understands voice, video, images, and text in a multimodal way. Need help planning a trip or coding a function? Gemini gives exact results with reasoning.

3. Microsoft Copilot (Bing AI)

Copilot is now baked into Windows and Bing. It offers real-time search results, integrated with Office apps, and powered by GPT-4. It helps users write, analyze data, and even generate charts based on queries.

4. Perplexity AI

A focused research-oriented engine that cites all sources. Great for students, professionals, and journalists who need quick summaries and trusted references. You can even ask follow-up questions naturally.

5. You.com

You.com is fully customizable, allowing users to select which sources and AI models to prioritize. It offers web, code, and image search, all in a scrollable chat-like interface.

6. Andi

A minimalist AI search tool designed to feel more like talking to a friend than using Google. It skips ads, focuses on clarity, and uses NLP to deliver just what you need.
